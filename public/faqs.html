Frequently Asked Questions

Q. What is Empower Her workshop?

A. Empower Her is a workshop designed to provide women with valuable skills and resources to enhance their personal and professional lives.

Q. Who can attend Empower Her sessions?

A. Empower Her sessions are open to women of all backgrounds and ages who are seeking empowerment and personal development opportunities.

Q. What topics are covered in the workshop?

A. The workshop covers a range of topics including self-care, confidence-building, goal-setting, mindfulness practices, and career development.

Q. How long are the Empower Her sessions?

A. The duration of each Empower Her session may vary, but typically ranges from one to two hours.

Q. Do I need to bring anything to the workshop?

A. Participants may be advised to bring a journal or notebook for note-taking, and comfortable clothing for any physical activities such as yoga or meditation.

Q. Are Empower Her sessions suitable for beginners?

A. Yes, Empower Her sessions are designed to be inclusive and welcoming to individuals at all levels of experience.

Q. Can I host an Empower Her workshop for my company or organization?

A. Yes, Empower Her workshops can be customized and tailored to the specific needs of your company or organization. Please contact me to discuss your requirements and pricing options.
